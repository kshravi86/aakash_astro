Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-24

Overview

This app allows users to generate astrology charts locally on their device. It does not require network access to function and does not send personal data to any server.

Data We Collect

- Optional name: If you enter a name, it is used only to label the generated chart on your device. It is not transmitted or shared.
- Birth details: Date, time, and place that you input are used only to compute the chart locally on your device. They are not transmitted or shared.

Data Processing and Storage

- All computations occur on the device using locally stored ephemeris files.
- The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
- The app does not access precise or approximate device location; city is selected from a built‑in offline list.
- The app does not collect or share the Android advertising ID.
- Uninstalling the app removes any locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the developer.
