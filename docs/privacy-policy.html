Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-09-24

Vedic Light is an offline astrology app. Your inputs (name, date, time, place) are used only on your device to compute charts. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any server.

Information We Process

  • Optional name you enter to label the chart.
  • Birth details: date, time, and birthplace you enter to compute the chart.

This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted off the device.

How We Use Information

  • Compute and display astrology charts and dasha based on your inputs.
  • Improve on-device experience (e.g., displaying your selected place and time).

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The app does not use analytics or advertising SDKs and does not collect an advertising ID.

Data Storage & Retention

  • Inputs are used in-memory to perform calculations. Ephemeris files are stored locally for offline computation.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data stored on the device.

Permissions

The app does not request location, contacts, camera, microphone, or storage permissions. It functions fully offline.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Components

The app uses Swiss Ephemeris for astronomical calculations. All computations occur on-device using locally bundled ephemeris data. Please see the Swiss Ephemeris license for its terms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page with the updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer.
